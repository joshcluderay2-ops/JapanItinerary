Japan Itinerary: Whisky, Anime & Nightlife

Base 1: Tokyo

Dates: Nov 10–18 (8 nights)

Hotel: $1174 (Booked)

Primary Stay Area: Shinjuku, Ikebukuro, or Akihabara

Interests

  • Anime
  • Bars & Nightlife
  • Whisky day trips
  • Akihabara

Suggested Highlights

  • Nightlife: Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Golden Gai for bars and food
  • Anime: Akihabara (Anime stores, arcades, figure shops)
  • Sights: TeamLabs Planets, Odaiba, Nakano Broadway
  • Food: Ramen Street at Tokyo Station, izakayas in Koenji or Shimokitazawa

Whisky & Drink-focused

  • Day trip to Kirin Fuji-Gotemba Distillery (Mt. Fuji area)
  • Day trip to Hakushu Distillery (Yamanashi, forest distillery)
  • Optional: Nikka Miyagikyo (Sendai, longer trip)

Base 2: Kyoto / Osaka

Dates: Nov 18–25 (7 nights)

Hotel: $892 (Booked)

Primary Stay Area: Gion or near Kyoto Station

Interests

  • Whisky
  • Temples & Autumn leaves
  • Traditional Japan
  • Osaka nightlife

Suggested Day Trips

  • Yamazaki Distillery (20–30 min from Kyoto)
  • Osaka (Dotonbori food scene, Nipponbashi anime area)
  • Nara (deer, Todai-ji)
  • Kobe (beef, sake brewery tours)
  • Uji (matcha + temples)

Drinks

  • Pontocho alley for cocktails
  • Kyoto whisky bars
  • Bar-hopping in Osaka

Base 3: Sapporo

Dates: Nov 25–30 (5 nights)

Hotel: $761 (Booked)

Primary Stay Area: Susukino (for nightlife) or Odori Park area

Interests

  • Whisky
  • Winter food
  • Local bars
  • Snow vibes

Highlights

  • Day trip to Nikka Yoichi Distillery (1 hr train)
  • Visit Otaru (historic canal town, seafood)
  • Mt. Moiwa Ropeway (night views over snowy city)
  • Sapporo Beer Museum & Beer Hall
  • Explore Susukino nightlife (bars, whisky lounges, ramen alley)

Food

  • Soup curry
  • Miso ramen
  • Snow crab
  • Jingisukan (grilled lamb)

Travel Plan

Travel Between Bases

Segment How Notes
Tokyo → Osaka Shinkansen (2.5 hrs) Covered by JR Pass
Osaka → Sapporo Flight (2 hrs) Fly from Itami/Kansai to CTS
Sapporo → Tokyo Flight (1.5 hrs) For return flight home

Flights

  • Osaka - Sapporo: Roughly $150. Use Peach, Jetstar, or ANA.
  • Sapporo - Tokyo: Return flight to Narita before flying home.

JR Pass

  • Get a 7-day pass, activate from Nov 10–17 for Tokyo–Kyoto and local day trips.
